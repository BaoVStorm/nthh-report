## General Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**

The working environment at FCJ is very friendly, open, and comfortable. The team members are energetic, enthusiastic, and always willing to support me whenever I face difficulties, even outside working hours. The workspace is neat and professional, which creates a pleasant atmosphere and helps me stay focused during my internship.

In addition, I can feel the closeness among team members through the way everyone communicates, guides, and supports each other at work. However, I think it would be even better if there were more casual sharing sessions, conversations, or team lunches so that interns and team members could get to know each other better and build stronger connections.

**2. Support from Mentor / Admin Team**

My mentor is always willing to support me, explain things I do not understand, and encourage me to ask questions whenever I encounter difficulties. During my internship, my mentor not only guided me in technical aspects but also shared practical experiences, helping me better understand how to apply knowledge to real work.

The admin team also provided great support with procedures, documents, and necessary information, creating favorable conditions for me to complete my tasks during the internship. Thanks to the support from my mentor and the admin team, I felt more confident in learning and carrying out the assigned tasks.

**3. Relevance Between Work and Academic Major**

The tasks assigned to me were quite relevant to my major and the knowledge I had learned at university. Fundamental knowledge in information technology, databases, data processing, and information systems helped me approach the work more easily.

Besides that, the internship also helped me expand my knowledge in new areas, especially AWS services, data pipeline development, data processing, and data visualization. As a result, I was able to strengthen my academic knowledge while also gaining exposure to a real working environment and technologies currently used in businesses.

**4. Opportunities for Learning and Skill Development**

During the internship, I had the opportunity to learn and develop many new skills. In addition to technical knowledge related to AWS, Data Engineering, and dashboards, I also learned how to use project management tools, track work progress, and present results more clearly.

Besides technical skills, I also improved my teamwork skills, communication skills in a corporate environment, and ability to receive feedback from my mentor. The practical sharing from my mentor and team members helped me gain a clearer view of my future career direction.

**5. Company Culture and Team Spirit**

The working culture at FCJ is very positive. Everyone respects one another, works seriously, but still maintains a friendly and cheerful atmosphere during discussions. Whenever there is an issue or a task that needs support, team members are always willing to help each other regardless of position or role.

Team spirit is one of the things that impressed me the most during my internship. When there is a project or task that needs to be completed, everyone works together, shares experience, and supports one another to achieve the best result.

**6. Policies / Benefits for Interns**

The company provides good conditions for interns during the working process, especially in terms of flexible time when necessary. This helped me balance my internship, study, and personal responsibilities.

In addition, being able to participate in workshops, events, and knowledge-sharing activities brought many valuable experiences. Through these activities, I had more opportunities to learn new knowledge, broaden my perspective on technology, and better understand the real working environment.

## Other Questions

**What were you most satisfied with during your internship?**

What I was most satisfied with during my internship was the friendly working environment and the enthusiastic support from everyone. Whenever I faced difficulties while working on tasks, I always received clear guidance and explanations from my mentor as well as other team members.

Besides that, I was also very satisfied with the opportunity to work with real technologies, especially AWS services. The internship helped me better understand how a data system is built and operated, giving me practical experience beyond the knowledge I had learned at university.

**Would you like to continue this program in the future?**

Yes. I would like to have the opportunity to continue participating in this program in the future if possible. The internship helped me learn a lot of new knowledge, improve my technical skills, and gain practical experience in a professional working environment.

I feel that the internship program brought me many valuable benefits. It not only helped me develop technically but also helped me improve my working attitude, communication skills, and ability to collaborate with others. These are very important experiences for my future career direction.
